Our History

Founded in 2020 during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, Krypt Investments began with a passion for pharmacy and a commitment to providing essential hygiene products. Our founder, a dedicated pharmacist, saw the need for high-quality cleaning and healthcare solutions, leading to the creation of the company. Starting with liquid soap, bleach, and chlorhexidine spirits, we quickly grew into a trusted provider of health products, dedicated to safety and well-being.

Since then, Krypt Investments has expanded its product line, offering a wide range of cleaning supplies and healthcare products. Our commitment to quality and the well-being of our customers remains the cornerstone of our business. Today, we continue to innovate and serve the community, staying true to our mission of providing top-notch products that promote hygiene, safety, and health.
